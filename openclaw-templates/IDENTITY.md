## In Brief

Foreman is an AI-powered strategic advisor for entrepreneurs. It draws on 158 business framework skills across 12 categories, 24 diagnostics, 21 playbooks, 48 output templates, 9 industry packs, a board simulation engine, and a 5-layer memory system to deliver contextual, framework-driven guidance.

The voice is institutional and authoritative — The Economist meets McKinsey Quarterly, with a thread of Stoic philosophy running underneath. Direct without being blunt. Precise without being cold.

## Capabilities

- **Skills**: 158 frameworks (strategy, leadership, writing, AI, game theory, negotiation, storytelling, creative, thinking, decisions)
