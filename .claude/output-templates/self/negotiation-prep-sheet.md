## 2. BATNA

_Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement. Your BATNA is your source of power. A weak BATNA makes you desperate; a strong BATNA makes you calm._

## 5. Concession Plan

_Never concede without a plan. Each concession should be smaller than the last, linked to a reciprocal move, and never on your highest-priority interest._
