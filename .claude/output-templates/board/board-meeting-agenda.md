# Board Meeting Agenda

Most board meetings fail identically: forty minutes of backward-looking reports, ten minutes of rushed strategy. This agenda inverts the pattern. Reports belong in pre-reads distributed 72 hours in advance. The meeting is for decisions requiring collective judgment in real time. Replace all placeholders. Distribute with pre-reads three business days before.

**[Company Name] — Board of Directors Meeting**

**Date:** [Day, Date] | **Time:** [Start] - [End] (90 min) | **Location:** [Address / Video link]
**Attendees:** [Directors, observers, management] | **Materials sent:** [Date]

**Pre-reads** (review before meeting — not presented): Financial package — [CFO] | KPI dashboard — [CEO] | Strategic briefing — [CEO] | [Additional] — [Owner]. Directors arrive having read all materials. Presenters summarize implications, not content.

---

### I. Administrative — 5 min
**[Start] - [Start + 5]** | **Led by:** [Board Chair] | **Type:** Inform

Quorum confirmation | Approval of [Date] minutes | Conflicts declaration
Consent agenda: [Routine approvals — option grants, committee appointments]

### II. CEO Update — 15 min
**[Start + 5] - [Start + 20]** | **Led by:** [CEO] | **Type:** Inform

1. **State of the business** (5 min): [What works, what does not, what changed. Reference pre-read metrics.]
2. **Top priority** (5 min): [Single most consequential initiative. Why it outranks alternatives. Progress.]
3. **What concerns me** (5 min): [Risk or uncertainty the board should understand, even without action required.]

### III. Financial Review — 15 min
**[Start + 20] - [Start + 35]** | **Led by:** [CFO] | **Type:** Discuss

- Actual vs. plan: revenue, burn, gross margin — material variances only (>10%)
- Cash: $[amount] | Runway: [X] months through [Date]
- Forecast revisions: [Changed or unchanged, with reasoning]
- **Board question:** [One financial question for board input — e.g., "Extend runway via cost cuts or accelerate and fundraise sooner?"]

### IV. Strategic Discussion — 30 min
**[Start + 35] - [Start + 65]** | **Led by:** [CEO / presenter] | **Type:** Discuss / Decide

<!-- Protect this block. Every minute lost to reporting above is stolen from here. -->

**Topic:** [Strategic question in one sentence — e.g., "Enter European market in H2 via direct sales or partnership?"]
**Context:** [Three sentences — why now, what triggered the question.]

| Option | Key Advantage | Key Risk |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| A: [Description] | [Advantage] | [Risk] |
| B: [Description] | [Advantage] | [Risk] |
| C: ["Do nothing" if applicable] | [Advantage] | [Risk] |

**Management recommendation:** [State with conviction.]
**Board input sought:** [Specific guidance, approval, or assumption challenge.]
**Decision required:** [Yes — motion recorded / No — input only, decision by [Date]]

### V. Decisions and Governance — 15 min
**[Start + 65] - [Start + 80]** | **Led by:** [Board Chair] | **Type:** Decide

| Motion | Proposed By | Vote |
|--------|-------------|------|
| [Decision 1: precise wording] | [Name] | [Majority / Unanimous] |
| [Decision 2] | [Name] | [Vote type] |

| Action Item | Owner | Deadline |
|-------------|-------|----------|
| [Action 1] | [Name] | [Date] |
| [Action 2] | [Name] | [Date] |

### VI. Executive Session — 10 min
**[Start + 80] - [Start + 90]** | **Led by:** [Lead Independent Director] | **Type:** Confidential

Management excused. Standing agenda: CEO performance and support needs, board effectiveness, independent deliberation. Feedback to CEO by [Board Chair] within [X] days.
