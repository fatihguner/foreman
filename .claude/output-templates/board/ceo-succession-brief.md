# CEO & Critical Role Succession Brief

Succession planning is the governance responsibility boards most frequently defer and most deeply regret. A departing CEO with no identified successor costs 5-10% of market value during transition — at a startup, where leadership concentration runs extreme, the figure can be terminal. Fill with honest assessments. A flattering plan protects no one.
