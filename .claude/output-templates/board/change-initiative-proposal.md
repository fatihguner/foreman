## 3. Change Roadmap

Eight phases, informed by Kotter's model. Each states its objective, key actions, duration, and the gate signal indicating readiness to proceed.

| Phase | Objective | Key Actions | Duration | Gate Signal |
|-------|-----------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **1. Establish Urgency** | [Authentic urgency, not performative] | [Data sharing, customer evidence sessions] | Wks [X-Y] | [Measurable proxy for urgency landing] |
| **2. Build Guiding Coalition** | [Assemble cross-functional leadership] | [Name the roles and why each is critical] | Wks [X-Y] | [Coalition formed, charter agreed] |
| **3. Develop Vision & Initiatives** | [Translate platform into owned strategy] | [Scenario workshops, initiative design] | Wks [X-Y] | [Vision ratified, backlog prioritised] |
| **4. Communicate for Buy-In** | [Every stakeholder understands why, what, and their role] | [Channels, cadence, feedback loops] | Wks [X-Y] | [Pulse survey confirms awareness threshold] |
| **5. Remove Barriers** | [Dismantle structural and cultural obstacles] | [Policy changes, training, role clarity] | Wks [X-Y] | [Top obstacles resolved or escalated] |
| **6. Generate Short-Term Wins** | [Visible results validating direction] | [Define wins in advance — accidental wins lack credibility] | Wks [X-Y] | [Wins documented and communicated] |
| **7. Sustain Acceleration** | [Use wins to drive deeper change] | [Expand scope, reinforce through systems] | Wks [X-Y] | [Metrics show sustained trajectory] |
| **8. Institute in Culture** | [Embed change as default, not mandate] | [Hiring criteria, promotions, rituals, rewards] | Ongoing | [Behaviours persist without programme reinforcement] |
