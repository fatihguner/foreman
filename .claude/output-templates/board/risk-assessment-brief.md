# Risk Assessment Brief

This briefing adopts the structure used by intelligence agencies when presenting strategic risk to senior decision-makers: ranked, concise, actionable. Each risk entry requires a narrative — probability/impact scores without context are theatre, not risk management. Replace all bracketed placeholders and delete this paragraph before distribution.
