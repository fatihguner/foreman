### Hypothesis

_One sentence. What we believe to be true that, if confirmed, justifies this initiative._

> [We believe that (target customer) will (adopt / pay for / switch to) (proposed solution) because (specific reason), resulting in (measurable business outcome).]

_What we know today that supports this hypothesis. Distinguish between hard data and informed assumptions. Be honest about the gaps._

**What we assume (not yet validated):**

### Experiment

_What we will build, launch, or test to validate the hypothesis. Describe the minimum viable version, not the full vision._

**What:** [Describe the experiment -- MVP, pilot, prototype, landing page, concierge test, etc.]

**Who:** [Target segment for the experiment -- be specific about the cohort]

**How:** [Distribution / delivery method -- how will the target segment encounter the experiment?]

**Duration:** [Time-boxed length of the experiment]

### Kill Criteria

_The conditions under which this initiative stops, regardless of sunk cost or emotional attachment. Defining these in advance protects the organization from escalation of commitment._
