## Part 2: Your Agenda (10 min)

**Feedback (SBI):** Situation: [When/where] | Behavior: [What you observed] | Impact: [Effect] | Direction: [ ] Reinforce [ ] Redirect

**Alignment:**
- **Priority next week:** [What and why]
- **Deprioritize:** [Explicitly named]
- **Context needed:** [Decisions or information from above]

**Development** _(one question per meeting, rotate quarterly):_
- [ ] What skill do you most want to build in 90 days?
- [ ] What energizes you? What drains you?
- [ ] Where do you want to be in one year?
- [ ] What project would you like to take on?
- [ ] What should I do differently as your manager?

**Dev notes:** [Substance of the answer -- feeds quarterly planning]
