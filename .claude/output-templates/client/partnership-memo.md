## 1. Shared Opportunity

<!-- Open with what BOTH organizations gain. Not what you want from them.
     The reader should finish this section thinking "they understand our
     position, not just their own." -->

[Three to four paragraphs describing the market opportunity, customer need, or strategic gap that neither organization can fully capture alone. Address:]

- The opportunity and its scale (quantified where possible -- market size, customer segment, revenue potential, competitive timing)
- Why this opportunity requires partnership rather than independent pursuit
- What happens if neither party acts (the cost of inaction for both sides)
- Why now -- the timing catalyst that makes this conversation urgent rather than theoretical

**The partnership thesis in one sentence:**

> [e.g., "By combining [Your org]'s distribution reach with [Partner]'s technical platform, we can capture [X% / $X] of the [specific market segment] within [timeframe] -- an outcome neither organization can achieve independently."]

## 6. Governance

_How decisions get made. How disputes get resolved. The governance structure you skip in enthusiasm is the structure you need in conflict._

**Decision authority:**

| Decision type | Authority | Approval required |
|--------------|-----------|------------------|

| Joint marketing and positioning | [Named role from each side] | [Mutual approval] |

| Scope changes or new investment | [Steering committee] | [Unanimous] |
| Partnership renewal or termination | [Executive sponsors] | [Per exit provisions below] |

**Governance structure:**

- **Executive sponsors:** [Name/Title from each org -- ultimate accountability]
- **Operating leads:** [Name/Title from each org -- day-to-day management]
- **Steering committee:** [Composition] -- meets [frequency]

**Dispute resolution:**

## 8. Exit Provisions

_Every partnership needs a clear, dignified exit path. Discussing exit terms at the outset is not pessimism -- it is the foundation of trust._

**Term:** [Initial duration -- e.g., "24 months from launch date"]

**Post-termination obligations:**
