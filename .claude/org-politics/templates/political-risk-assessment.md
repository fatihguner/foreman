## Ethical Check

**Transparency test:** Would you be comfortable if every stakeholder saw this risk assessment?

- [ ] Risks are assessed honestly -- not inflated to justify inaction or deflated to avoid uncomfortable truths
- [ ] Mitigation strategies use legitimate influence, not manipulation or coercion
- [ ] Contingency plans respect stakeholder dignity -- no retaliation for opposition
- [ ] Kill triggers are genuine -- you will actually stop if reached, not move the goalposts

**If any box is unchecked, revise the assessment before acting on it.**
