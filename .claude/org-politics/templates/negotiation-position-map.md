## Ethical Check
**Transparency test:** Would you be comfortable if every party saw this preparation document?

- [ ] Party profiles are honest assessment, not caricature or wishful thinking
- [ ] Concession architecture seeks genuine mutual benefit, not exploitation of information asymmetry
- [ ] Sequencing serves efficiency, not divide-and-conquer manipulation
- [ ] Red lines are respected -- no plans to pressure past non-negotiables
- [ ] Win conditions are genuine -- seeking a deal where every party gains

**If any box is unchecked, revise the strategy before entering the negotiation.**
