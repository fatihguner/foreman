## Ethical Check

**Transparency test:** Would you be comfortable if every stakeholder saw every other stakeholder's message?

- [ ] Every message is truthful -- different emphasis is acceptable, different facts are not
- [ ] No stakeholder is told something that contradicts what another is told
- [ ] Sequencing serves strategic clarity, not information manipulation
- [ ] Feedback channels are genuine, not performative
- [ ] The "What NOT to Say" column reflects sensitivity, not concealment
